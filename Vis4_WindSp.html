Wind Speed


Wind speeds in most cities are around 0 – 15 miles per hour. Fewer and more fewer cities have wind speeds between 15 – 20 and 20 – 25 miles per hour, respectively. Only a few cities around the equator to 25-degree latitude north have wind speeds of 25 or higher miles per hour.
